Who I am

and what I do

Date: January 1st, 2020

I am a Chinese Canadian studying physics (minor) and electrical engineering (major) at the University of British Columbia.

I have loved physics since I was 15 and the inquisitive attitude that practicing physics bring. Math is interesting too but it ain't nearly as cool!

Before 15, I spent my time world-building and story telling. I have (terribly) written over 600 pages of stories that took place in my fantasy world. My passion for writing manifests itself today as an indispensable tool for reasoning, figuring things out, reflecting, organizing and planning.

Electrical engineering is amazing. It shows how smart people took the physics we knew, simplified it in a clever way, then combined them into machinery that aids the practical man. Engineering requires execution, and execution hinges on a lot of information. I find organizing information to be a joy.
